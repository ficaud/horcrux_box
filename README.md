# Horcrux Box

## WARNING This is a work in progress. The hardware and software are not yet ready for production use.

A device that uses a local, easy-to-use Shamir's Secret Sharing scheme to generate or assemble sensitive information.

Features that make it better than dropping secrets in cloud storage or relying on a website that may go out of business in the future:

- **Local operation**: Everything is done disconnected from the internet, so you don't have to worry about your secrets being stolen by hackers.
- **Easy to use**: The device is designed with a user-friendly captive portal to generate and assemble secrets.
- **Longevity**: The device is designed to last for years. You don't need to worry about a website you depend on closing or going out of business. Everything is yours, and you have full control over it.
- **Open source**: The device is open source, so you can verify that it does what it says—and nothing more. You can also modify it to your needs.
- **DIY**: The device is designed to be built by anyone with basic electronics skills. You can build it yourself or buy a pre-built one.
